Projects

Yelp Dataset Challenge

Rating prediction is an important aspect for any product or service providing business, as accurately predicting the rating a customer would give to a product leads to better personalized recommendations, which leads to higher conversion rates for the business. In this project I participated in the yearly contest by Yelp, which challenges users to use their massive dataset and come up with creative improvements for their business. I created a latent-factor model, which accurately predicts user ratings with a RMSE of 0.9.

Tinderbot

In this project I created a generative conversational bot trained on conversations from the popular dating platform Tinder. The challenging part of the project was creating the dataset, which was done by scraping Tinder related images from the web, training a CNN classifier to separate the images of conversations from all other images, using OCR to extract the text from the images, and finally pre-processing the text so the bot can be trained.
